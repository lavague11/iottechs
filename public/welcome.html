Your installation is complete. Here's everything you need to get the most out of it.

Getting started

1
Download the viewing app. Your technician installed and linked your cameras to the mobile app on-site. Search your app store for the NVR brand shown on your recorder, or ask us and we'll send the exact link.
2
Sign in. Use the username and password set up during installation. If you didn't write it down, contact us and we'll help you recover it.
3
View live & recorded footage. Tap any camera for a live feed; scrub the timeline to review recordings. Most systems retain ~30 days of footage.
4
Set up motion alerts. Enable push notifications in the app so you're alerted to motion in the zones that matter.

Good to know

Your recorder needs power & internet. Keep the NVR and router plugged in — remote viewing relies on both.
Clean lenses occasionally. A soft, dry cloth keeps the picture sharp; avoid harsh cleaners.
Workmanship warranty. Your installation is covered — if anything isn't working right, reach out and we'll make it right.
Want to expand? Adding cameras later is easy. Start another project from your dashboard any time.
