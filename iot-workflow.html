Customer

Online Intake Form

Prospect submits site details, scope, and contact info through the public web form. Lead is captured into the CRM with source, temperature, and status auto-set — ready for the rep to work.

Email + TextTo customer: "Thanks — we've received your request and a specialist will reach out shortly."  · Internal alert to sales rep.
2
SalesAdmin

Qualify & Site Assessment

Lead is assigned to a sales rep, temperature set, qualified, and a walkthrough / virtual assessment is scheduled to scope cameras, NVR, cabling, and access. Status → Contacted.

Email + TextTo customer: "Your site assessment is confirmed for [date / time]."  · Reminder sent 24 hrs & 1 hr before.
3
SalesSystem

Proposal Generated & Sent

VAULT DARK proposal built from the locked 13-element format with line items, PCP discount, and payment structure. Owned by the assigned rep, emailed to the customer for e-signature. Status → Quoted · auto-moves to Follow-Up if unsigned.

Email + TextTo customer: "Your proposal is ready to review & sign."  · Auto follow-up if unsigned after 48 hrs.
Customer Decision — Approve?
✕ Declined / Revise

Returns to the rep for revision or re-quote. Stays Follow-Up — or Closed with a reason (Price, Competitor, Ghosted…).

✓ Approved & Signed

Deposit invoice triggers · rep commission flagged. Status → Approved.

02

Deposit, Work Order & Dispatch

4
SystemCustomer

Collect Deposit

Immediately on approval, the deposit invoice is sent and collected (Zelle / card / certified check). Deposit clears before the job is scheduled — no deposit, no work order.

Email + TextTo customer: "Deposit invoice — pay to lock in your install."  · Receipt auto-sent once paid.
5
System

Work Order Auto-Created

Once the deposit clears, the approved proposal converts directly into a work order — scope, equipment list, site address, and schedule window carried over with no re-entry.

EmailInternal alert to dispatch: new work order ready to assign.
6
Admin

Assigned to Technician

Dispatcher assigns the job to an available tech (Sam, Wessam, Charlie) based on skillset, location, and calendar.

TextTo technician: "New job assigned — tap to review & accept."  · Push to tech portal.
Technician — Accept Job?
✕ Declined

Returns to dispatch queue for reassignment.

✓ Accepted

Job locks to tech. Equipment is pulled from inventory.

7
SystemCustomer

Appointment Scheduled & Confirmed

Install date/time is booked against the tech's calendar and offered to the customer. Customer confirms or reschedules from the Customer Portal — the slot syncs to the tech, admin, and customer calendars automatically. Status → Scheduled.

Email + TextTo customer: "Your installation is confirmed for [date · window]. Reply or tap to reschedule."  · Auto reminders 24 hrs & 1 hr before · "Tech en route" text on the day.
03

Execution & QC

8
Technician

Job Workflow / Build-Out

Tech works the guided checklist — mounting, cabling, NVR config, audio, access control — logging photos and notes at each step from the field app. Status → In Progress.

TextTo customer: "Your technician has started — here's their name & live status."  · Tech check-in / check-out timestamps logged.
On-Site — Additional Scope Found?
Job Addendum

Tech discovers extra work beyond the original scope — added camera, more cable/conduit, a switch, unforeseen labor. Instead of stopping the job, an addendum captures it.

Tech logs addendumAdds line items, qty, photos, reason from the field app → Customer approvesMini-proposal e-signed for the added scope & cost → System updatesWork order, equipment pull & billing revised automatically
↻ Approved addendum merges into the active work order — tech resumes build-out · feeds Job Cost Analysis
9
QC

Quality Control Review

Completed checklist + photos reviewed against scope. Punch-list items returned to tech if needed; otherwise the job is cleared for the customer walkthrough.

TextInternal: QC passed — schedule the final walkthrough with the customer.
04

Closeout — Customer

10
TechnicianCustomer

Final Walkthrough

Tech walks the customer through the finished system on-site — every camera, the NVR, app access, and any addendum work. Customer signs off that the install meets scope.

Email + TextTo customer: "Walkthrough complete — please confirm you're satisfied & e-sign acceptance."
11
SystemCustomer

Final Payment Collected

Balance-due invoice is presented and the remaining balance is collected (Zelle / card / certified check). Payment must clear before handover — the system is not handed over until paid in full.

Email + TextTo customer: "Final invoice — pay your balance to complete the job."  · Auto past-due reminders until paid.
Paid in Full?
✕ Not Paid

No handover. Balance stays open with reminders; system access held until cleared.

✓ Paid

Proceed to system handover & certificate.

12
TechnicianSystem

System Handover

Customer receives full ownership — login credentials, mobile-app setup, admin access, recorded-footage retrieval, and a quick training on day-to-day use.

EmailTo customer: "Your system is all yours — credentials, app setup & quick-start guide."
13
System

Completion Certificate Issued

Final step — the customer receives a branded job-completion certificate with system summary and warranty terms. Status → Completed → Closed (reason: Completed).

EmailTo customer: "Your completion certificate & warranty — plus a quick review request."  · Review request prompts referrals.
