What We Do

One team for the entire low-voltage stack.

From the camera on the wall to the cable in the ceiling — designed, installed, and supported by IOT TECHS.

Security Cameras & CCTV

High-res IP and analog camera systems with NVR recording, night vision, and live viewing from anywhere.

Commercial Audio

Background music, zoned paging, and overhead speakers tuned for restaurants, retail, and offices.

Networking & Cat6

Structured cabling, network switches, and rock-solid Wi-Fi built to carry every device on your site.

Toast & POS Cabling

Clean, dedicated data and power runs for Toast and other POS systems — terminals, KDS, and printers wired right so your registers never drop.

Access & Door Control

Keypads, smart locks, intercoms, and door entry systems that keep your space secure and in your control.

NVR & Storage

Rack-mounted recorders, RAID storage, and retention planning so the footage is there when you need it.

24/7 Monitoring

Professional 24/7 monitoring that dispatches police, fire, and EMS the moment an alarm triggers — so help is on the way fast.

AI Camera Development

Smart cameras that do more than record — people counting, object & intrusion detection, and custom AI alerts built to your site's needs.

LPR & Automation

License plate recognition for gates, parking, and access — automatically log vehicles, trigger entry, and flag watch-list plates in real time.

Security at your fingertips.

Every system we install is built to work together and managed from a single app on your phone or desktop. Modern hardware, clean cabling, and a team that picks up the phone.
